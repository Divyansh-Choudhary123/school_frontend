Change is indeed a fundamental characteristic of our era, one that we witness in every passing generation and prominently within school community. With each academic year, our school expands in both size and scope, welcoming new students while bidding farewell to seniors who venture forth into the world.

However, amidst this whirlwind of change, it's crucial to acknowledge that it can often be challenging. The constant flux of teachers, the intricacies of evolving syllabi, the departure of familiar faces, and the arrival of new peers can all contribute to feelings of stress and pressure among students.

Yet, it's essential to recognize that change is not only normal but also an invaluable learning experience. It moulds us, shapes our perspectives, and prepares us for the unpredictable journey of life. However, to navigate through these changes successfully, one must have a solid foundation of constructive goals.



Our school provides just that—a nurturing environment that instils belief and encourages students to strive towards their objectives. With determination and resilience, success becomes attainable. However, it's essential to remember that success is not handed out freely; it's earned through hard work and perseverance.

As we embark on a new academic session, I extend my heartfelt wishes to all students. May you embrace the challenges that come your way, armed with the knowledge that your efforts will pave the path to success. Best of luck to each and every one of you as you stand on the threshold of the last academic session (2023-24) with your back towards the past and open arms to embrace the new.

About us

Five decades ago Shri Bishal Chand Jain, a great philanthropist, prominent social worker and educationist & his wife Smt. Sushila Jain realised the need of quality education in a community that was increasingly becoming aware of the rapidly changing times and the need to adapt them. The school became functional on 1st July 1966 under the management of a Committee comprising of noted educationists and eminent personalities. Asha Modern School is the enterprise and endeavour of the Founder Principal and currently the Manager Miss Asha Jain who realized the need to provide meaningful education to a large number of children. With her visionary approach and lofty ideas she has truly made Asha Modern School a ‘Citadel of Learning’. It is now a premium institution providing quality education to the children.

The school is situated near the Civil Court. Housed in the most ultra-modern building, it is well equipped with laboratories, library, gymnasium, canteen etc. A team of highly qualified teachers constitute the teaching faculty and provide the highest quality of education.

The School caters to pupils from classes Nursery to XII, preparing students for All India Secondary School Certificate Examination and the All India Senior School Certificate Examination of the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi.

It is the aim of the school to make learning a joyous experience through discovery, questioning and independent research. The academic year begins in the first week of April every year and final exams are conducted in early March of the following year.

The subjects taught up to Class X are: English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry & Biology), Social Studies (History, Civics, Economics and Geography), Computer Science, Drawing, S.U.P.W. and P.Ed.
